In 2022 I read 22 books. I track all the books I read on [my reading
list](/reading/). Here are the ones I recommend:

## Fiction

- Robert Harris, Dictator
- Lustrum, Robert Harris
- World War Z, Max Brooks
- Imperium, Robert Harris
- A Dance with Dragons, George R R Martin
- The Hobbit, J.R.R. Tolkien
- The Handmaid’s Tail, Margaret Atwood
- A Feast for Crows, George R R Martin

## Non-fiction

- The War of the World, Niall Ferguson
- Only the Paranoid Survive, Andy Grove
- A Promised Land, Barack Obama
- Mao’s Great Famine, Frank Dikötter
- The end of the world is just the beginning, Peter Zeihan
- The Fate of Rome, Kyle Harper
- The Changing World Order, Ray Dalio
- The Coddling of the American Mind, Greg Lukianoff and Jonathan Haidt
- Matt Ridley, How Innovation Works
- The Power Law, Sebastian Mallaby
